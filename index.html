What are Piles or Hemorrhoids?

Hemorrhoids, also known as piles, are swollen veins in the lower rectum and anus. They cause discomfort, pain, itching, and bleeding. They may occur due to prolonged sitting, constipation, or straining during bowel movements. Based on severity, they are classified into four grades.

What is Laser Treatment for Piles?

Laser treatment is a modern, minimally invasive method to treat piles. It uses focused laser beams to shrink or cut hemorrhoidal tissues with high precision. It causes less pain, quick healing, and minimal complications compared to conventional surgeries.

Types (Grades) of Piles

Piles are classified into four grades based on the severity and prolapse of the hemorrhoids:

  • Grade I: Small inflammations inside the anal lining, not visible externally.
  • Grade II: Prolapse occurs during bowel movement but returns back on its own.
  • Grade III: Prolapsed piles that can be pushed back manually into the anus.
  • Grade IV: Large, painful piles that remain outside the anus and cannot be pushed back.
